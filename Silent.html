Silent Hills Officially Launches on Mobile

By Alex | April 30, 2025

Silent Hills, the critically acclaimed indie title, has finally arrived on mobile. Starting today, Android and iOS users can experience the hand-drawn puzzle adventure that previously charmed the PC gaming community.

With a unique visual style, emotionally driven storytelling, and bizarre yet lovable characters—including a talking dog—Silent Hills offers a blend of mystery and humor. The mobile version keeps the full content of the original, with optimized touch controls and smooth performance on most devices.

Players will explore surreal landscapes, interact with memorable NPCs, and solve puzzles that challenge logic and perception, all from the comfort of their mobile screens.

Silent Hills is now available for $10.99 on the Google Play Store.
