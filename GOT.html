Netmarble’s Game of Thrones: Kingsroad Launches May 21 — Pre-Registration Now Live

By Alex | April 30, 2025

Fans of Westeros have something big to look forward to this spring. Netmarble has officially announced that Game of Thrones: Kingsroad, its highly anticipated strategy-RPG mobile title, will launch globally on May 21, 2025. Even better — pre-registration is live now on both the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

A Return to Westeros, Reimagined

Kingsroad is set during a time of deep conflict in the Seven Kingdoms, allowing players to step into the shoes of powerful lords and commanders vying for the Iron Throne. While based on the world of George R.R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire, the game introduces an original narrative path tailored for mobile gameplay.

Players will manage their own house, forge alliances, command armies, and face threats from both rival factions and iconic creatures — including white walkers and dragons.

Gameplay: Strategy Meets RPG

  • Hero Collection & Progression: Recruit characters both new and familiar from the GoT universe.
  • Dynamic PvP & PvE Battles: Engage in large-scale battles across the map, including sieges and skirmishes.
  • Alliance Systems: Team up with other players for territory control and kingdom domination.
  • Expansive World Map: Explore major locations like Winterfell, King’s Landing, and The Wall.

Stunning Visuals & Lore-Driven Design

The game promises console-quality visuals optimized for mobile. From the icy North to the deserts of Dorne, Kingsroad aims to immerse players in a living, breathing Westeros.

Netmarble, known for titles like Marvel Future Fight and The Seven Deadly Sins: Grand Cross, has partnered closely with HBO and Warner Bros. to ensure authenticity and lore accuracy.

Pre-Registration Rewards

Players who pre-register now will receive exclusive in-game rewards at launch, including:

  • 5-star Hero Summon Ticket
  • 1,000 Gold
  • Custom "House Banner" Cosmetic

You can pre-register through:
- Google Play Store
- Apple App Store
- Official Website

Final Thoughts

With the success of other franchise-based mobile games, Game of Thrones: Kingsroad could be one of 2025’s biggest launches. The combination of deep strategy, fan-favorite lore, and polished mobile experience sets the stage for a compelling return to Westeros.
