FAU-G Relaunches with Major Overhaul on May 1, 2025

By Alex | May 1, 2025

FAU-G Domination Multiplayer has officially relaunched today with a major overhaul, offering gamers a brand-new experience on Android and iOS platforms. Developed by nCORE Games, the reboot aims to redeem its mixed past with polished gameplay, a new storyline, and advanced combat mechanics.

The 2025 version introduces a full multiplayer experience alongside its upgraded campaign. Set in futuristic conflict zones, players will engage in high-stakes missions inspired by real-life military operations, now enhanced with better AI and immersive sound design.

Multiplayer Modes in FAU-G 2025

  • Team Deathmatch (TDM): Two teams of 5 players each battle in fast-paced matches. The first team to reach the kill limit wins.
  • Free For All (FFA): Every soldier for themselves. Up to 10 players fight in a survival-style match where the top scorer wins.
  • Control Point: Teams must capture and hold strategic zones across the map to earn points and win.
  • Private Rooms: Custom lobbies allow friends to host matches with personalized rules and maps.

These multiplayer additions are supported by improved netcode, low-latency matchmaking, and anti-cheat features, making FAU-G more competitive and community-friendly than ever before.

FAU-G now also supports competitive ranking, controller compatibility, and optimized performance for low-end devices, making it accessible to a wide range of players across India and beyond.

FAU-G is available for free on the Google Play Store
