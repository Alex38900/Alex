Valorant Mobile: Upcoming Chinese Beta Test Details Revealed

By Alex | April 29, 2025

Exciting news for Valorant fans! Riot Games is officially preparing to launch a **closed beta test** for Valorant Mobile exclusively in China. The announcement, made through Chinese social platforms and Tencent’s official Valorant portal, has sparked massive excitement across the community.

What We Know So Far

  • Launch Date: Expected mid-May 2025
  • Eligibility: Chinese mobile users (via pre-registration)
  • Platforms: Android and iOS
  • Progress Reset: Yes, all data will reset post-beta

The beta will test core mechanics, touch controls, and server performance before expanding to other regions. Players will experience familiar agents, maps, and abilities in a format tailored for mobile gameplay.

Will It Come Globally?

While Riot hasn’t confirmed a global beta yet, developers hinted at staged regional testing. Global players should stay alert for beta announcements in their country soon.

Final Thoughts

Valorant Mobile's China beta will be a critical step toward a full launch. With tight controls, agent balance, and Riot’s dedication to mobile adaptation, the game is shaping up to be a serious contender in the mobile eSports arena.

Stay tuned for more updates and hands-on impressions as soon as the beta goes live!
